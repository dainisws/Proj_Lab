Documentation

1) Menu Generator Inputs

To use the menu generator you have to be registered. Menu generator is available here. There you will be able to choose the store, minimum and maximum nutrients, specify taste for foods or food groups, choose how much you want to prioritize price over taste or the other way around. To rate foods give them ratings from 1 to 10, you can also give a rating for a food group, this will set the rating to all related foods that haven't been rated yet. Finally click "Compute" (It might take a second).

2) Menu Generator Results

After entering the inputs and clicking "Compute" you should see a list of optimal items and a list of all the other items. The optimal results will have a set amount of grams you should pick, however you can change these values. After you add the item to your cart the results will be recalculated. You can view all the items in your cart by clicking the cart icon on the top right. There you will be able to remove items from the cart and return to the results page. If you wish to restart the process just visit the menu generator here by opening the Menu Generator section.

3) Registration

For the sake of keeping your food preferences saved you have to be registered to use our services. Enter a unique username, unique email and a strong password. This information will not be visible to other people. After registering you need to log in. To view your profile click on the top right profile image icon, there you will be able to log out, delete account and change your profile picture.
